# Roadmap

## Phase 1 — Foundation
- establish the public product narrative
- define the core study workflows
- publish architecture and design docs

## Phase 2 — Practice and review
- add paper and CBT-style practice experiences
- support structured notes and flashcards
- improve revision loops and memory workflows

## Phase 3 — Intelligence
- introduce adaptive recommendations
- improve analytics and readiness tracking
- support smarter learning assistance

## Phase 4 — Collaboration
- support tutor and classroom workflows
- enable shared study environments
- expand multi-user and team features
