# 🎓 Supported Examinations

ExamFlowOS currently supports multiple entrance and competitive examinations.

## Available

- AP ECET
- TG ECET
- AP ICET
- TG ICET
- AP POLYCET
- TG POLYCET

## Organization

Every examination follows the same hierarchy:

Examination

↓

Year

↓

Branch / Shift

↓

Paper

↓

View Paper

↓

Download PDF

↓

CBT

Additional examinations will continue to be added in future releases.
