# 📚 Phase 1 — General

---

## What is ExamFlowOS?

ExamFlowOS is a modern examination preparation platform designed to bring every essential study tool into one organized workspace.

Instead of switching between multiple websites, PDF repositories, timers, note-taking applications, and revision tools, students can prepare using a single platform that combines previous year question papers, Computer-Based Tests (CBT), flashcards, active recall, focus tools, productivity statistics, and more.

The goal is simple:

> **Help students spend less time managing resources and more time learning.**

---

## Who created ExamFlowOS and why?

ExamFlowOS was created by **Devi Charan** after experiencing how fragmented exam preparation can be.

Students often spend more time searching for papers, organizing PDFs, managing notes, and switching between different applications than actually studying.

The vision behind ExamFlowOS is to bring everything together into one modern, fast, and student-focused learning platform.

---

## Is ExamFlowOS free to use?

Yes.

Core educational resources are intended to remain freely accessible.

Students can browse supported examinations, access available previous year papers, use supported CBT modules, and benefit from the platform's learning tools without unnecessary barriers.

As ExamFlowOS grows, additional optional features may be introduced while keeping the core learning experience accessible.

---

## Which students is ExamFlowOS designed for?

ExamFlowOS is designed primarily for students preparing for competitive examinations, especially those conducted in Andhra Pradesh and Telangana.

Current public support includes:

- AP ECET
- TG ECET
- AP ICET
- TG ICET
- AP POLYCET
- TG POLYCET

Support for additional examinations is planned as the platform evolves.

---

## What makes ExamFlowOS different from other exam preparation websites?

Most examination websites focus only on providing downloadable PDFs.

ExamFlowOS provides a complete preparation workflow.

Instead of simply downloading a paper, students can:

- Browse previous year papers
- Preview papers online
- Download original PDFs
- Attempt CBT versions
- Create flashcards
- Review using Active Recall
- Track study statistics
- Stay focused with Focus Mode

Everything is connected into one learning experience.

---

## Is ExamFlowOS an official government or university platform?

No.

ExamFlowOS is an independent educational platform.

It is not affiliated with any government department, university, examination authority, or board unless explicitly stated.

Students should always verify official examination notifications, schedules, and announcements through the respective examination authorities.

Future updates may include additional explanations and educational resources.

Rather than being just another paper repository, ExamFlowOS aims to become a complete operating system for examination preparation where every learning activity is connected.

# 🚀 Getting Started

---

## How do I start using ExamFlowOS for the first time?

Getting started is simple.

1. Open the ExamFlowOS website.
2. Choose your examination.
3. Browse available previous year papers.
4. Open a paper using the integrated Paper Viewer.
5. Download the PDF or start a CBT session where available.
6. Continue your preparation using flashcards, recall sessions, and Focus Mode.

No complicated setup is required to begin exploring the platform.

---

## Do I need an account?

Many parts of ExamFlowOS can be explored without signing in.

However, creating an account unlocks features that require personal data storage, such as progress tracking, flashcards, recall history, statistics, and synchronization across devices.

---

## Which browsers are supported?

ExamFlowOS is designed for modern browsers that support Progressive Web Apps and current web standards.

Recommended browsers include:

- Google Chrome
- Microsoft Edge
- Mozilla Firefox
- Safari (latest versions)

Using an up-to-date browser provides the best experience.

---

## How do I choose my examination?

From the home page, navigate to the Examinations section and select the examination you are preparing for.

Each examination contains its own organized collection of papers, subjects, and learning resources.

---

## Can I switch examinations later?

Yes.

You can switch between supported examinations at any time without reinstalling the platform.

Your study experience remains organized independently for each supported examination.

---

## Is there an onboarding tutorial?

The interface is intentionally designed to be simple and intuitive.

As the platform evolves, optional guided tours and onboarding experiences may be introduced to help first-time users explore features more quickly.

---

## How do I find previous year papers?

Navigate to:

**Home → Examination → Year → Branch/Shift → Paper**

This consistent structure helps students locate papers quickly across all supported examinations.

---

## Should I begin with papers or CBT?

For most students, the recommended workflow is:

1. Review previous year papers.
2. Understand the examination pattern.
3. Practice using the CBT mode.
4. Analyze results.
5. Revise using flashcards and Active Recall.

This sequence provides both understanding and practical exam experience.

---

## Can I install ExamFlowOS?

Yes.

ExamFlowOS supports installation as a Progressive Web Application (PWA) on compatible browsers.

Once installed, it behaves much like a native application while continuing to receive updates through the web.

---

## Is there a demo mode?

The platform is designed so students can explore available content before committing to a structured study routine.

Additional guided demonstrations and onboarding experiences may be introduced in future updates.

# 👤 Account

---

## Do I need to create an account to use ExamFlowOS?

No.

Many parts of ExamFlowOS are available without creating an account. You can browse supported examinations, explore available previous year papers, preview PDFs, and discover the platform before signing in.

Creating an account becomes valuable when you want to save personal study data, synchronize progress, and access personalized features.

---

## What are the benefits of creating an account?

Creating an account allows ExamFlowOS to personalize your learning experience.

Benefits include:

- Save study progress
- Synchronize data across devices
- Store flashcards
- Track Active Recall history
- Save CBT attempts
- View long-term statistics
- Backup learning data
- Restore progress after changing devices

Your account helps transform ExamFlowOS from a paper library into a personalized study platform.

---

## Can I use ExamFlowOS without signing in?

Yes.

Guest access is supported for many browsing features.

However, guest sessions may not retain personal learning data such as flashcards, statistics, recall schedules, or CBT history after the session ends.

For the best experience, signing in is recommended.

---

## Which sign-in methods are supported?

Supported authentication methods depend on the current release.

When available, ExamFlowOS may support:

- Email and Password
- Google Sign-In
- Additional authentication providers in future releases

Always use the official website to create or access your account.

---

## Is my study progress synchronized across devices?

Yes.

When signed in, supported study data is synchronized with your account, allowing you to continue learning across compatible devices.

Examples include:

- Flashcards
- Recall schedules
- CBT history
- Study statistics
- Preferences

Synchronization requires an active internet connection.

---

## What happens if I study without an account?

Without an account, you can still explore many platform features.

However, personal learning data may remain only on the current device and could be lost if browser data is cleared or the application is reinstalled.

Creating an account helps preserve your progress.

---

## How do I reset my password?

If password-based authentication is available:

1. Open the sign-in page.
2. Select **Forgot Password**.
3. Enter your registered email address.
4. Follow the instructions sent to your inbox.
5. Create a new secure password.

If you signed in using Google or another external provider, password management is handled by that provider.

---

## Can I change my registered email address?

Yes.

When supported, account settings allow you to update your registered email address.

Some changes may require email verification for security purposes.

---

## Can I permanently delete my account?

Yes.

Students should have control over their own data.

When account deletion is available, ExamFlowOS will provide a process for permanently removing your account and associated personal information, subject to applicable legal or operational requirements.

Always review the latest Privacy Policy before requesting deletion.

---

## Can I log in on multiple devices?

Yes.

Depending on the authentication system and security policies, you can generally access your account from multiple compatible devices.

Examples include:

- Desktop computer
- Laptop
- Tablet
- Mobile phone

Synchronization keeps your supported study data consistent across devices.

---

## Is there a limit on the number of devices I can use?

Current versions of ExamFlowOS are designed for personal educational use.

Reasonable multi-device usage is supported.

Future releases may introduce device management features that allow users to review and manage active sessions.

---

## Can I merge two accounts?

Account merging is not currently supported.

If duplicate accounts are created accidentally, future versions may provide migration or merge tools.

Until then, it is recommended to continue using a single primary account.

---

## What personal information does ExamFlowOS require?

Only the information necessary to provide account-related services is collected.

Depending on the authentication method, this may include:

- Name
- Email address
- Authentication credentials
- Basic profile information

ExamFlowOS does not request unnecessary personal information.

---

## How is my account information protected?

Protecting student information is an important design principle.

ExamFlowOS uses secure communication (HTTPS) and modern authentication practices to help safeguard user accounts.

Security measures continue to evolve alongside the platform.

---

## Can I use the same account after changing phones or computers?

Yes.

Simply sign in using the same account on your new device.

Once synchronization completes, your supported learning data and preferences will become available.

---

## What should I do if I cannot sign in?

If you're unable to access your account:

1. Verify your email address.
2. Check your password.
3. Ensure your internet connection is working.
4. Reset your password if necessary.
5. Try another supported browser.
6. Contact support if the issue continues.

Most login issues can be resolved by following these steps.

---

## Does ExamFlowOS verify email addresses?

When email-based accounts are used, verification may be required to improve account security and reduce unauthorized access.

Verification procedures may change as authentication features evolve.

---

## Can I use one account for multiple examinations?

Yes.

One account can be used to study across all supported examinations.

Your papers, flashcards, CBT history, and statistics remain organized according to each examination, helping you switch between preparation goals without creating separate accounts.

---

## Will I lose my progress if I uninstall the PWA?

If your data has been synchronized to your account, simply signing in again after reinstalling will restore supported information.

If you used the platform entirely offline without synchronization, locally stored data may not be recoverable.

For this reason, signing in regularly is recommended.

---

## Where can I manage my account settings?

Account-related options can be accessed from the **Settings** section of ExamFlowOS.

Depending on the current release, you may be able to manage:

- Profile information
- Authentication settings
- Study preferences
- Notification preferences
- Connected devices
- Privacy options
